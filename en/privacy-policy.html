Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2, 2025

Introduction

Analog Clock Pro ("the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

This App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data is stored only on your device.

Information Collection

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Photos or media files
  • Device identifiers

Information Stored on Your Device

The following information is stored only on your device and is never transmitted externally:

  • App settings (theme selection, display settings)
  • Custom watch design data
  • Language settings
  • Purchase information (Pro version unlock status)

In-App Purchases

This App offers in-app purchases through Apple's App Store. All payment processing is handled by Apple, and we do not have access to your payment information.

Third-Party Data Sharing

This App does not share, sell, or provide your data to any third parties.

Data Protection

Data created in this App (such as custom watches) is protected by iOS's standard data protection features. Data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted over the internet.

Children's Privacy

This App does not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13. Since this App does not collect personal information, it is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as necessary. Significant changes will be notified on this page. We recommend checking this page periodically.
